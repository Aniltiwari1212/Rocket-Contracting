Hail Damage

Having a roof inspection quickly after a storm is important. The longer you wait to submit your claim and schedule your roof inspection, the more difficult it can be for an insurance adjuster to see hail impacts on your roof, making it difficult to get the coverage you need. Our roofing professionals are highly knowledgeable when it comes to identifying and documenting residential roof and commercial roof hail damage and experts at restoring a property to its pre storm condition.

In short, getting your roof inspection done quickly will give your insurance company less to question about the cause of the roof damage than if you waited six months or more to submit your hail damage roof claim.
